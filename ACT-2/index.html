Greenland: the Ice Kingdom of the World

Some of their tourist attractions are...

Ilulissat Icefjord

"This massive and fast-moving glacier travels more than 100 feet per day, dropping huge icebergs into its narrow coastal inlets."

Prince Christian Sound

"The Prince Christian Sound is a waterway in Southern Greenland."

World of Greenland

"World of Greenland is a Greenlandic company with deep roots in Ilulissat tourism."

Greenland National Museum and Archives

"Greenland National Museum is Greenland's largest cultural history museum. The Museum preserves the Greenlandic history and our objects, art and images are from all over the country."

Nuuk Water Taxi

"The Nuuk Water Taxi is a transportation service in Nuuk, Greenland's capital. It offers a scenic and efficient way to travel between neighborhoods, attractions, and remote areas accessible by water."

Eqip Sermia Glacier

" Eqip Sermia Glacier, or Eqip Glacier, is a notable glacier in western Greenland near Ilulissat. It's famous for its dramatic calving events, where large ice chunks break off and create stunning icebergs."

Sermermiut

" Sermermiut is a historical Inuit settlement located near Ilulissat, Greenland. It offers stunning views of the Ilulissat Icefjord, a UNESCO World Heritage site."

Qornok

" It was inhabited until the early 20th century and offers a glimpse into traditional Inuit life. The area is known for its scenic views of the surrounding landscape and icebergs. Today, visitors can see the ruins of old homes and learn about the history and culture of the Inuit people who lived there.."

Nanortalik Open Air Museum

" The Nanortalik Open Air Museum is an outdoor museum in Nanortalik, Greenland. It showcases traditional Inuit and Greenlandic culture through historical buildings and artifacts. "
