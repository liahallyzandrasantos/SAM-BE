Welcome to the Land of Fae!

How It Started:

Avalon was founded first on E9 Server, 4 months ago.
Avalon E10 later followed the first one after a month.
Date: September 2024

Guild Target:

The guild's only target initially is to create a safe space for beginners.
Although, being competitive turns out to be fun too.
Current Status: Active Guild to both GoC/Ragna and Fishing.

Guild Theme:

The guild's lore is based on the game Fate:Grand Order Lostbelt.
Avalon le Fay, the land of Faeries.
Note: Avalon E9 follows the original lore, E10 became a roleplay guild.

Warning:

The guild Avalon has two guilds to exercise their roles well. Avalon E9 are much more content-friendly.
Avalon E10, as part of roleplaying; people may curse in-game. Please do think first before joining E10 Avalon.

So what is Avalon?

Avalon is a Guild usually created for fishing activities only.

The Captain Morgan le Fay created this as an act of de-stressing through her main servers, and also brought Habetrot (Sage) to teach her The two became interested in being competitive to the new server and thus test how well they can lead new players of the game. They might encounter some stressing problems with in-game and in real life, but they continued to guide their beloved 'faeries' to get their best experiences in the game. They also met other people who are willing to learn to lead like them. They created another branch on another server and they are both doing well in managing the two guilds. Some people might left, but some people also still comes to their wonderful guild.

Currently, the Avalon Guilds are both 4 months old.
